Tauranga

About

Tauranga, situated on New Zealand's North Island, is a coastal gem that blends natural beauty with cultural delights, offering a diverse range of experiences for visitors.

Tauranga, with its blend of natural wonders, culinary delights, and cinematic magic at Hobbiton, captures the essence of New Zealand's diverse attractions. Whether chasing waterfalls at Kaiate Falls, savoring flavors at Lantern, or stepping into the fantastical realm of Hobbiton, Tauranga offers a tapestry of experiences that reflect the rich tapestry of the region.

Kaiate Falls
Landmark

Kaiate Falls stands as a breathtaking natural attraction near Tauranga. Tucked away in the lush greenery, the falls cascade down in a mesmerizing display, creating a serene and picturesque setting. The walking trails leading to the falls provide a tranquil escape, inviting nature enthusiasts to immerse themselves in the beauty of New Zealand's landscape.

Hobbiton Movie Set
Cultural Icon

A short drive from Tauranga leads to the iconic Hobbiton Movie Set, a must-visit for fans of J.R.R. Tolkien's Middle-earth. This meticulously crafted set, nestled in the rolling hills of Matamata, brings the fantasy world to life. Guided tours offer a captivating journey through hobbit holes, lush gardens, and the enchanting landscapes that served as the backdrop for the beloved "Lord of the Rings" and "Hobbit" film trilogies.

Lantern
Gastronomic Experience

Nestled in the heart of Tauranga, Lantern is a culinary haven celebrated for its diverse menu that gracefully fuses Asian, Thai, Vietnamese, Malaysian, and Healthy Fusion cuisines. This restaurant artfully combines traditional New Zealand ingredients with international influences, creating a symphony of flavors that tantalizes the taste buds.

The warm ambiance of Lantern, paired with its commitment to using fresh, local produce, makes it a cherished choice for those seeking an exquisite and evolving culinary experience in the vibrant city of Tauranga. From aromatic Thai dishes to the vibrant flavors of Vietnamese cuisine, Lantern captures the essence of Tauranga's multicultural culinary landscape.
